# Session 2 — DM Play Notes
*Raw notes taken during play. Archived for continuity reference.*

---

## Cart Repair

Players investigated the cart and its isopod. Rear axle bent, front bearing broken, supplies strewn about.

Nancy found a pretty straight piece of leaf to use as a replacement axle. They packed the bearing with grease — then decided to just pull the damaged wheel. End result: a tri-wheeled cart, underloaded but functional.

Drix wrote a note and tucked it in the cargo: *"Wanted to make sure that these supplies made it back to the city, bandits :(" — Drix*

Party elected to swap pack animals: Bessie (the RJS sowbug) is now attached to the cart heading home. They're keeping Bestie (the pillbug from the wagon) to travel with them.

## Camp — Night 1

Party picked campsite 2: between the roots, with the old fire ring.

## Backstory — Nancy

During camp, Nancy's player shared backstory: Nancy was hired to steal something from a wizard. Instead of stabbing the evil wizard, he got caught. It took 14 months to escape. He carved his way out with a spoon. Still got the job done.

Thoraxville came up in this conversation — Nancy mentioned it in passing. **Flag: needs development.**

## The Tube

Party opened T'wigfingers' sealed tube. They were expecting something exciting. They got DATA. Tabby and Nancy started on a map, noting the direction of the glow.

---

## Burnt Village

Party arrived at the burnt village. Found Lars (the earwig who ran in Session 1) — visibly brain-borked, huddled in the remains. He told them what he knew:

- Two types of ants: black and red.
- Bodies were dragged in a northeastish direction.

Party marked the direction on the map. They left Lars behind with the supplies he already had from the raid. No additional supplies were given; no attempt was made to bring him along. The moment was quiet. Nobody said anything about it.

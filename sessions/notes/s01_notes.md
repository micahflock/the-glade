## Other Notes from Play

- Drix performed on the walnut shell lute for Bessie in the stables. She warmed to him.
- Crisp and cold — unseasonable chill in the air as they loaded up Bessie and headed out.
- Nancy listened for news near the city gate. Heard about dead farms, people getting desperate.
- Party did NOT stop at the market stalls. Drix played a song on the road and earned a small amount of coin.
- Marching order leaving Chitinholm: Tabby (front, 5–10 ft ahead), then Drix, then Nancy, then Bessie.

## Combat Notes (Earwig Bandits)

- Drix yelled at the bandits first — took two rocks before the fight fully broke out.
- Tabby critted a dagger stab early.
- Nancy's *Sleep* (23 HP) dropped one earwig for 10 rounds.
- Drix's *Dissonant Whispers* drove the leader off — leader came back.
- Tabby applied *Hunter's Mark* on the leader.
- Drix used *Hold Person* on the leader.
- Nancy landed the killing blow on the leader. The other pillaging earwig fled instantly.
- Rest of bandits scattered.
- Bert ran past the party without stopping — one pack in hand, straight back to Chitinholm.

## Naming Note

DM notes use "Bessie" for both the sowbug (from RJS stables) and the pillbug (from Bert's cart, later named Bestie) at various points. The 20 HP reference in combat notes refers to the pillbug (Bestie in registry). Sowbug (Bessie) has no HP recorded and was sent back with the cart.

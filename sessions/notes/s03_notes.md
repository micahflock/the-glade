# Session 3 — DM & Player Notes
*Compiled from DM play notes — Session 3*

---

## Pre-Session / Opening

Nancy spent the night at the Antennington Amber Mine picking Garett's brain about the Bloom and its magical properties. Garett built a small box/housing for the Ahganmin that makes changes in the amber's glow easier to observe. The party made breakfast before departing.

Bestie was hitched and brought along for the journey.

## Player Attitudes & Character Moments

- **Nancy (ROWYN):** Scared, but interested in learning new magical knowledge. Extended conversation with Garett shows Nancy leaning into the arcane curiosity side of the character.
- **Drix (Katie):** "Hell yeah, I'm in the story, I will have shit to sing about." Fully bought into the narrative. Plans to use Disguise Self as a fire ant for the Winter Farm infiltration — showing creative spell use.
- **Tabby (Heather):** Overwhelmed by the triangulation math ("MATHS INNIT") but enjoying the group dynamic. Continuing to carry the navigation/map role.

## Tactical Decisions

- Party chose to leave the road and travel ~60 feet into the forest to parallel the path from cover after discovering ant tracks.
- Drix cast Locate Animals or Plants (2nd-level spell) to confirm ants ahead before engaging — smart use of resources.
- Leafcutters fought against the party halfheartedly on the fire ants' orders; as combat turned, some switched sides and joined the party. All fire ants killed, no leafcutter casualties.
- Nancy's direct approach to the loyalist was a strong RP moment — "might makes right doesn't sit with me" — but didn't win him over.

## Pacing

Session covered less ground than planned. The entire Winter Farm arc (approach, interior, fungus chambers, Grainmother Vell and the rebel cell meeting) is now Session 4 material. This gives Session 4 a strong cold open with the infiltration plan already in place.

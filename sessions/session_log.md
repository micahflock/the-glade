## Session 0 — The Beginning

The party assembled at the Chitinholm branch of the Royal Jelly Society, summoned by a general call to bugs of action. In a cramped upstairs room repurposed as a war room, they were briefed by the Sub-Committee on Impeding Winter Doom: Francesca "Boodle" Booloticia IV (merchant guild, jewel beetle), Dr. T'wigfingers (scientist, stickbug), and Sven (#7834, ant drone). The committee presented their theory: the unnatural cold gripping the Glade is not weather — it is a shockwave expanding from a single epicenter. Boodle's tip came from missing trade shipments; T'wigfingers had cross-referenced refugee data to establish directionality; Sven reported collapsed agricultural yields and raiding parties. The party was tasked with traveling to a lost RJS branch to retrieve records that, combined with Chitinholm's data, would triangulate a heading toward the source. In exchange: RJS membership, a title, and the legend. The session ended with the party moving through Chitinholm's market square.

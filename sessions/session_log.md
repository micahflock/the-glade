# The Glade — Session Log

## Session 0 — The Beginning


The party assembled at the Chitinholm branch of the Royal Jelly Society, summoned by a general call to bugs of action. In a cramped upstairs room repurposed as a war room, they were briefed by the Sub-Committee on Impeding Winter Doom: Francesca "Boodle" Booloticia IV (merchant guild, jewel beetle), Dr. T'wigfingers (scientist, stickbug), and Sven (#7834, ant drone). The committee presented their theory: the unnatural cold gripping the Glade is not weather — it is a shockwave expanding from a single epicenter. Boodle's tip came from missing trade shipments; T'wigfingers had cross-referenced refugee data to establish directionality; Sven reported collapsed agricultural yields and raiding parties. The party was tasked with traveling to a lost RJS branch to retrieve records that, combined with Chitinholm's data, would triangulate a heading toward the source. In exchange: RJS membership, a title, and the legend. The session ended with the party moving through Chitinholm's market square.

## Session 1 — On the Road

Boodle made her exit immediately, leaving T'wigfingers to brief the party and hand off the sealed triangulation tube. After a pass through the RJS stockroom and stables — where Drix won over Bessie the sowbug with a walnut-shell lute performance — the party loaded up and headed east through Chitinholm. They passed through the packed market square without stopping, Nancy caught talk of dead farms near the gate, and Drix earned a coin or two busking on the road out. On the frozen road east, they ran into a family of shield bugs fleeing toward Chitinholm and then a cart under attack by earwig bandits. Drix called the bandits out first and took two rocks for it. The fight ended with Tabby's crits, Nancy's Sleep spell, Drix's Dissonant Whispers and Hold Person, and Nancy landing the killing blow on the leader — the rest scattered. The cart driver Bert ran past the party without a word, straight back to Chitinholm. The pillbug still hitched to the wagon didn't bolt — just watched. The party kept her.

## Session 2 — The Broken Wagon

The party repaired the damaged cart with a scavenged leaf for an axle, packed the bearing with grease, and pulled the broken wheel entirely — leaving a functional tri-wheeled rig. They swapped pack animals: Bessie was hitched to the repaired cart with a note from Drix tucked in the cargo, sent back toward Chitinholm on her own. Bestie, the pillbug from the wagon, came with the party. Camp was made between the root flares off the road; Tabby scouted, Nancy scoped the trail from high ground, Drix played lute and settled the site. The party finally opened T'wigfingers' sealed tube — data, not drama — and Tabby and Nancy began a physical map. The next day brought the burnt village: no bodies, no food, only soot and Lars, the earwig who had run in Session 1, now fractured and barely present. He named two ant types — black and red — and pointed them northeast, where the bodies were dragged. The party left him with what he already had and moved on. Antennington was ransacked but standing; the RJS building yielded a note in green ink pinned with a hand-pickaxe, and Nancy found the Tune Hammer of Amber in the lab. At the Antennington Amber Mine, Ricko the ox beetle ran the lift, and Garett the green weevil waited on Level 2 with his triangulation parchment and a glowing magic amber rock — Ahganmin — which he pressed into Nancy's hands. The party left the mine with three data sources now combined on their map and a heading toward the Bloom's source.

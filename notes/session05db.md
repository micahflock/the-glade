# Session 5 Debrief

Voice-recorded debrief on the drive home after session 5. Cleaned-up notes covering only what actually happened in session 5. Future ideation lives in `notes/session06ideas.md`.

## Overall

Session went great. The party got through pretty much everything we had planned for session 5.

## What we hit

### Canopia
- Party made it to Canopia.
- Made contact with **Gleam** (the beetle) and completed the dead drop.
- Gleam gave them some information, plus a vague promise to connect them to the **Leafcutter Queen**.
- Session ended partway through the conversation with Gleam.

### The Queen's Crown (casino)
- Party went to the Queen's Crown.
- They said they wanted to do a heist, then sat at a table.
- Initial idea: pickpocket. Pivoted to spreading a rumor that the ants are growing fungus off dead bodies.
- Audience was high-rolling wasps — the rumor didn't resonate. They didn't really care about groundling stuff.
- Party flubbed it enough that security escorted them out.
- They engaged with the heist concept and seemed to like it; left the door open to coming back for another attempt.

## What we did not hit

- They did not visit the **Royal Jelly Society**.
- We did not give Gleam much in the way of concrete next steps to share — we ran out of prepped material there.

## DM notes for follow-up

- We dropped a hint that there are mantises hunting bugs out in the overgrowth — they registered them as big scary enemies.
- Open thread: Gleam still owes them real direction toward the Leafcutter Queen. We need to actually write that.

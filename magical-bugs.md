## Fire Ant Overseers

Four variants of a fire ant "overseer" — the caste above commander, below
queen. Each is grounded in a real *Solenopsis* (or close kin) species. The
party is level 3 heading into the Winter Farm, so the **Winter** tier is the
most immediately relevant, but full gradients are laid out for future reuse.

---

### 🔥 Pheromone Crowner — Red Imported Fire Ant Major
*Solenopsis invicta* (Formicidae)

**Archetype:** Controller / Support — coordinates and buffs other ants

**Reference:** [AntWiki — Solenopsis invicta](https://www.antwiki.org/wiki/Solenopsis_invicta) · search "Solenopsis invicta major worker macro"

**Notable Features & Behaviors:**
- True polymorphic species — majors are larger than minors with broader heads and serve as defense and food-processing caste
- Communication is almost entirely chemical: trail pheromones, alarm pheromones, queen-presence cuticular hydrocarbons, recruitment piperidines
- A single major can pheromone-recruit a swarm to a target within seconds
- Cuticular signaling tells workers *who is in charge* — queen-status is broadcast as a chemical signature

**Magic Gradient Effects:**

**Winter** (party level ~3):
- Enhancement: Her pheromones carry a faint heat-shimmer; nearby workers visibly stiffen and move in unison when she gestures.
- Mechanic: **Crown-Scent (1/turn, bonus action).** One ally within 30 ft gains advantage on its next attack OR moves up to half its speed without provoking. Workers panic and scatter for 1 round if she dies.

**Spring** (party level ~6):
- Enhancement: Her antennae glow as she paints commands in the air. The scent-trails are visible.
- Mechanic: **Scorch-Trail (action, recharge 5–6).** Draws a 30-ft line of glowing pheromone. Allied ants moving along it gain +10 ft speed and +1d6 fire damage on their next attack. Enemies crossing the line make DC 13 CON save or take 2d6 fire and be marked (next attack against them is at advantage).

**Summer** (party level ~9):
- Enhancement: Her commands override free will. Lesser ants stop being individuals.
- Mechanic: **Hive Override (action, recharge 5–6).** Up to 6 allied ants within 60 ft each immediately take a reaction attack (+5, 2d6+3 piercing + 1d6 fire). Targets in 15-ft cone of her crown-scent: DC 14 WIS save or be **charmed** for 1 minute by the pheromone (treats her colony as friendly).

**Fall** (party level ~12):
- Enhancement: She is no longer "an ant" — she is a node in a singular will, projected outward.
- Mechanic: **Conflagration of Will (action, 1/day).** All ants within 120 ft act on her initiative for 1 minute and gain +2 to hit and +1d6 fire damage. Each non-ant in the area: DC 17 WIS save or be **dominated** for 1 round (repeats save end of turn); on success, take 6d6 psychic from the rejected command.

### 🪨 Skull-Cracker — Tropical Fire Ant Big-Head
*Solenopsis geminata* (Formicidae)

**Archetype:** Brute — slow, devastating melee

**Reference:** [AntWiki — Solenopsis geminata](https://antwiki.org/wiki/Solenopsis_geminata) · search "Solenopsis geminata major worker"

**Notable Features & Behaviors:**
- True major workers have *disproportionately* large boxy heads housing massive mandibular muscles
- Caste evolved specifically to crack hard seeds — grass, sorghum, maize — that minor workers can't process
- Slower, less mobile, but the colony's preferred bone-breaker; also defensive front-line
- Was the dominant New World fire ant before *S. invicta* displaced it — older lineage

**Magic Gradient Effects:**

**Winter** (party level ~3):
- Enhancement: His head has grown grotesquely large, mandibles glowing a dull cherry-red from within.
- Mechanic: **Crusher's Bite.** Melee +5, reach 5 ft, 2d8+3 piercing. On a hit, target's non-magical armor cracks: −1 AC until repaired, max −2. Designed to crack carapaces on the corpse line.

**Spring** (party level ~6):
- Enhancement: His mandibles are constantly forge-hot; chitin chars where he bites.
- Mechanic: **Sear-Snap (recharge 5–6).** Bite for 3d8+3 piercing + 2d6 fire. If target is **prone or grappled**, double the fire damage and the wound smolders (1d6 fire start of each of target's turns, DC 13 CON to end).

**Summer** (party level ~9):
- Enhancement: A single bite snaps a beam in half. He pulps what he holds.
- Mechanic: **Pulverizing Crush (action).** Grapple (athletics +9), then bite the grappled target for 4d10+4 piercing. Object or construct AC 20 or less: destroys it outright.

**Fall** (party level ~12):
- Enhancement: His head is the size of his body. His jaws don't close — they *consume*.
- Mechanic: **Mawcrusher (action, recharge 5–6).** 15-ft cone bite-engulf. DC 17 STR save or be **swallowed**: 6d6 fire + 4d10 piercing per turn, restrained, total cover from outside. Escape with DC 17 STR check or by dealing 30+ damage to the head from inside.

### 👁️ Eye-Stinger — Little Fire Ant Inspector
*Wasmannia auropunctata* (Formicidae)

**Archetype:** Skirmisher / Controller — small, mobile, debuff-focused. The "spy of the queen."

**Reference:** [Wikipedia — Electric ant](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electric_ant) · search "Wasmannia auropunctata macro"

**Notable Features & Behaviors:**
- Extremely small (1.0–1.5 mm in real life) but disproportionately painful sting
- **Documented to cause corneal blindness** in cattle, dogs, cats — stings to the eye produce keratopathy
- Forages arboreally — drops from leaves and branches onto targets below
- Forms massive supercolonies; thousands of workers operate as one diffuse intelligence

**Magic Gradient Effects:**

**Winter** (party level ~3):
- Enhancement: Smaller than the other ants, riding atop a worker like a lookout. Her sting glows white.
- Mechanic: **Pin-Sting.** Melee +6 (Finesse), 1d4+3 piercing. On hit, DC 12 CON save or target has **disadvantage on Perception (sight) checks** for 1 minute. Stacks up to 2× (full blindness).

**Spring** (party level ~6):
- Enhancement: She drops from above without warning. Her sting flashes like a struck match.
- Mechanic: **Drop-Strike (when target moves under her).** Reaction attack +7, 2d6+4 piercing + 1d6 radiant. DC 13 CON or **blinded** until end of target's next turn. **Inspector's Eyes:** allied ants within 60 ft can target enemies blinded by her without disadvantage from cover.

**Summer** (party level ~9):
- Enhancement: She doesn't drop — she falls in a slow shower of light, dozens of her at once.
- Mechanic: **Stinging Rain (action, recharge 5–6).** 20-ft radius, all creatures: DC 14 DEX (3d6 piercing, half on save) and DC 14 CON or **blinded for 1 minute** (save end of each turn). She has 3 "duplicates" — destroy one to dispel the swarm.

**Fall** (party level ~12):
- Enhancement: She is not one ant. The "Inspector" is a distributed mind — every ant in the chamber is her eyes.
- Mechanic: **The Queen Sees Through Me (passive).** While any ant in the encounter is alive, she has truesight and the queen knows everything they perceive. **Burning Glance (action, 1/day):** target one creature within 120 ft she can see — DC 17 CON save or **permanently blinded** until cured by *greater restoration*; on save, blinded 1 minute.

### 🛟 Pyre-Anchor — Black Fire Ant Furnace Veteran
*Solenopsis saevissima* (Formicidae)

**Archetype:** Sentinel / Mini-boss — anchors a position; physically links subordinates into a heated super-body

**Reference:** [Wikipedia — Solenopsis saevissima](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Solenopsis_saevissima) · [PNAS — Fire ants self-assemble into rafts](https://www.pnas.org/doi/10.1073/pnas.1016658108)

**Notable Features & Behaviors:**
- Documented self-assembly behavior: workers link bodies with mandibles and tarsi to form waterproof rafts and towers in floods
- Linked rafts behave as a single material — repel water, share load, redistribute heat
- South American native, older lineage, heavy venom load
- This is the answer to the open **Heated Body trait** question — the overseer is the prototype

**Magic Gradient Effects:**

**Winter** (party level ~3):
- Enhancement: His carapace radiates a visible heat-shimmer. Frost melts in a 5-ft ring around him.
- Mechanic: **Heated Body (passive).** A creature that touches him or hits him with a melee attack within 5 ft takes **1d4 fire**. He sheds bright light 5 ft / dim 10 ft. Resolves the open Heated Body question — propose: *contact damage, scaled by tier (1d4 / 2d6 / 3d6 / 5d6).*

**Spring** (party level ~6):
- Enhancement: He grabs nearby workers and they don't pull away — they *fuse* to him, smoldering.
- Mechanic: **Anchor-Link (bonus action).** Pull an ally ant within 5 ft into a body-link. Linked ant adds its HP to his and grants him +5 ft reach; while linked, both deal +1d6 fire on melee. Can hold 2 links. Heated Body damage rises to **2d6**.

**Summer** (party level ~9):
- Enhancement: Three workers, four, six — fused into a smoldering column with him at the head. The shape moves as one.
- Mechanic: **Pyre-Tower (action, 1/encounter).** Pull up to 6 ants within 30 ft into a 15-ft-tall fused tower. Tower: AC 17, total HP = sum of components, reach 10 ft, multiattack 3 bites (2d8+4 + 2d6 fire). 5-ft aura: DC 15 CON or 3d6 fire start of turn. Killed components shed off — tower shrinks accordingly.

**Fall** (party level ~12):
- Enhancement: The colony itself is his body. Every ant within sight will burn at his command — including itself.
- Mechanic: **Immolation Conscript (action, recharge 6).** Up to 8 ants within 60 ft *erupt into flame and rush a target.* DC 17 DEX save in 30-ft line per ant rushed: 4d6 fire each (the ants die, but he knows their addition). **Furnace Heart (passive):** when reduced below half HP, his Heated Body becomes a 15-ft aura (5d6 fire), and he can fuse with ANY corpse — including PCs and worksite victims — to extend his linked body.

## Campaign Connections (Fire Ant Overseers)

- **Winter Farm cold open (Session 4):** The Pheromone Crowner is the natural *first overseer* the party meets — she'd be running the body-cart unloading line, ordering leafcutters around. Her presence makes Drix's Disguise Self gambit *much* harder (cuticular hydrocarbon recognition).
- **Heated Body open question (`memory/Open_Questions.md` line 37):** The Pyre-Anchor's Winter mechanic is a clean proposal — *1d4 contact damage, scales 1d4 → 2d6 → 3d6 → 5d6 by tier*. Apply to all fire ants moving forward.
- **Loyalist liability:** The Skull-Cracker is the "punishment officer" archetype — if the Loyalist betrays the party, this is what gets called in. Visceral and personal.
- **Resistance inside the farm (Grainmother Vell):** The Eye-Stinger explains how the rebel cell stays so quiet — she's why Vell has built the resistance behind a *deliberate collapse* (out of sight = out of mind for the Inspector).
- **Fire ant queen prefiguration:** The Pyre-Anchor is a perfect Spring-tier preview of the queen — she fights similarly but at boss scale (consumes the entire farm into a single burning super-organism).
